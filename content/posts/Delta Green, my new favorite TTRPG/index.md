Ever since April of 2025, when my friend mentioned this TTRPG in my vicinity, I have slowly but surely been consumed by the Delta Green roleplaying game. It has quickly become my de facto roleplaying game. After finishing my campaign of IMPOSSIBLE LANDSCAPES, getting ready to start my new campaign, and writing my own short operation, I felt like I needed to spread the word about this fantastic system, so here I am.
<!--more-->
![TERROR](img/terror.png "Image credit: Arc Dream Publishing")

I started off playing TTRPGs with Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition and then moved on to other games like Pathfinder 2nd Edition on the fantasy side, but also experimenting with games like Mothership and Blades in the Dark for a different vibe. Thinking back, I have always loved horror elements in roleplaying games and I keep implementing them into the games I run. I've never ran Call of Cthulhu, but I have played in one short campaign... and I wasn't too big of a fan of it. It felt too pulpy for me, and honestly the subject matter was not that horrifying.
